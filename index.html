Today's reality

College degrees and grades do not suffice to assess a student or recent graduates potential job performance. Contrary to 5-10 years ago, it is now necessary to have other merits in addition to a credential or degree to prove one’s worth.
Such context makes recruiting college students both exhausting and chaotic for companies.

Using the current available data on a student (Resume, college degree and grades), companies can only guess if candidates “might potentially” be good at the job they are applying to.

Having no empirical data other than this can prove to be problematic for a company, and could result in a "Bad Hire", which on average costs a company 15 000 USD. Elevate ensures your ability to hire with confidence, knowing your new employees will perform.

A few words from our Founder Victor

"Elevate's goal is quite simple. We are looking to award an individual who has proven their worth, while also giving a company the opportunity to hire with full understanding of who and what they are getting, before they have it”.
